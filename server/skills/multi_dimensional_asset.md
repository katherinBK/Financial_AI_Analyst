**This skill enables an agent to proactively identify high-growth investment opportunities by blending fundamental criteria with technical milestones.**

* **CANSLIM Filtering Logic:** The agent applies the **CANSLIM** method to filter for "growth stocks," prioritizing market leaders with high market capitalization (>$80B), positive annual earnings growth over five years, and strong institutional ownership**.**
* **Milestone Monitoring:** The agent tracks **52-week High/Low** milestones**. When a stock breaks above its 52-week high, the agent interprets this as a potential breakout and a sign of strong upward momentum**.
* **Volume Validation:** To avoid "traps" or fake breakouts, the skill requires a mandatory volume confirmation; a valid signal must be accompanied by a significant spike in volume compared to previous days
